MarkHTML

This will help you change the Markdown text to HTML code in PHP.

Use

  1. Unzip your Zip and rename to Markhtml .
  2. Put your Markdown text in to $mark.
  3. Add something in your PHP document.
include "markhtml/markhtml.php"; //Head include

$html = markhtml($mark);
  1. Now your HTML text is in $html .
